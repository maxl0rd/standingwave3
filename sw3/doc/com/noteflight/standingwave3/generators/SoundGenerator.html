SoundGenerator()Constructor
public function SoundGenerator(sound:Sound, ad:AudioDescriptor = null)

A SoundGenerator serves as a source of sound extracted from an underlying Flash Player Sound object, usually a loaded mp3. Has an internal cache for the extracted sound, making it essential for wavetable usage.
