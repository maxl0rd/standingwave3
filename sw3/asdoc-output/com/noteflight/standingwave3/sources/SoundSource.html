Packagecom.noteflight.standingwave3.sources
Classpublic class SoundSource
InheritanceSoundSource AbstractSource Object
Implements IRandomAccessSource

A SoundSource serves as a source of stereo 44.1k sound extracted from an underlying Flash Player Sound object. To extract a sample at a lower resolution, or use the extracted sound as a wavetable, you should probably use SoundGenerator instead.
